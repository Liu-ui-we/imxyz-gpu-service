Frequently Asked Questions

How do you offer such low prices?

+

We leverage China's cloud providers (Aliyun, Tencent Cloud) which have significantly lower infrastructure costs and government subsidies. We purchase resources at local rates and pass the savings to international customers.

What about network latency?

+

Instances are located in China, so expect 150-250ms latency from North America/Europe. This is acceptable for batch processing, AI training, and development work. Not suitable for real-time applications.

Is my data secure?

+

We don't access your data. You get full SSH access to your instance. We recommend encrypting sensitive data and using VPNs. We don't store any of your data after instance termination.

Can I get a refund?

+

Yes, within the first 24 hours if the instance doesn't work as described. After that, we offer prorated refunds for unused time if you cancel early.
